## How to read the script tables

Each chapter is a 3-column table:

- **ON SCREEN** — the page or click the viewer should be seeing.
- **FACE CAM / B-ROLL** — when to be on camera vs. screen share.
- **WHAT YOU SAY** — read this out loud, close to word-for-word. Tone is energetic and friendly, not stiff.

`[bracketed italics]` are stage directions, not spoken.

## Recording conventions

- **Tone:** energetic and promotional, but still teaching. Smile, keep the pace up.
- **Two windows ready:** a clean recording browser logged out (for the signup chapter) and, if you want, a second pre-seeded landlord account so you skip empty states fast.
- **Before you hit record:** clear the browser of unrelated tabs, set zoom to ~100-110% so text is readable, hide bookmarks bar.
- **Money:** all amounts are NGN (₦). Say "naira" out loud.
- **Chapters:** the `⏱ CHAPTER` markers map 1:1 to YouTube chapter timestamps. Keep them so you can drop the master video into chapters and also cut standalone clips.
- **No DocuSeal / e-signature** in this product version. Tenancy agreements are uploaded/generated and attached only.
