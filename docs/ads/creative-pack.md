## Reel shot list 1 — DEMO (Ad B), 20-30s, vertical 9:16

Record on phone screen + a quick face intro. Burn captions in (most watch muted).

1. (0-3s) HOOK, face to camera, energetic:
   "If you manage property for different landlords, watch this."
2. (3-8s) Cut to screen: the manager dashboard. Voiceover:
   "Every landlord and every unit, one screen."
   On screen: scroll the property/unit list.
3. (8-15s) Show a payment being recorded, then the receipt appear. VO:
   "Record a payment, the receipt is made for you."
4. (15-22s) Show the "who's paid / who's owing" view + a renewal date. VO:
   "You always know who paid, who's owing, and what's due next."
5. (22-28s) Back to face. CTA:
   "It's free to start. Tap the button and message me, I'll set up your first
   property with you."

Tips: real data blurred where needed. Keep cursor movements slow. No music
louder than your voice.

## Reel shot list 2 — FOUNDER / ORIGIN (Ad C), 25-35s, vertical 9:16

Just you, phone at eye level, good light, talking straight to camera. Raw is fine.

1. (0-3s) HOOK:
   "Agents in Nigeria manage millions in rent with a notebook and WhatsApp."
2. (3-12s) PROBLEM:
   "One lost receipt, one forgotten renewal date, and your reputation takes the
   hit, even when you did nothing wrong. The landlord's trust is your whole
   business."
3. (12-22s) WHY YOU BUILT IT:
   "So we built Property360. One place to manage every landlord's property,
   record every payment, and keep the proof, automatically."
4. (22-30s) INVITE + CTA:
   "If you manage property for other people, message me. I'll show you your
   whole book in one place. It's free to start."

Tips: speak like you're talking to one person. Smile. One take, don't over-edit.
