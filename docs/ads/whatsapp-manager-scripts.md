# WhatsApp sales scripts — property managers & agents

For Click-to-WhatsApp ad replies. Goal of every chat: get them to a free-trial
signup at https://property360.africa?role=agent, or book a 10-minute screen-share
demo. Keep it human and fast. Reply within minutes when you can; speed closes.

Tone: warm, plain Nigerian-professional, never pushy. One question at a time.
No walls of text on WhatsApp. Voice notes work well for the demo offer.

---

## 0. The pre-filled opener (set this in the ad)
Ad CTA opens chat pre-filled with:
> "Hi, I manage property and I'd like to see how Property360 works."

## 1. First reply (send fast, within minutes)
> Hi 👋 thanks for reaching out. Glad to help.
>
> Quick one so I show you the right thing: roughly how many units do you manage
> right now, and for how many different landlords?

(If they opened without the pre-fill text, lead with: "Hi 👋 you reached
Property360. Are you managing property for landlords, or for yourself?")

## 2. Qualify (1-2 questions max, then move)
Listen for: portfolio size, number of landlords, and their biggest headache.
Follow-ups, pick one:
> Got it. And how do you track rent and receipts now: notebook, Excel, WhatsApp,
> or something else?

> When a landlord asks "who has paid this month," how long does it take you to
> answer them?

Use their answer as the hook in step 3.

## 3. Pitch back their pain (mirror, then the fix)
Template:
> That's exactly what Property360 fixes. You'd have all {N} units across your
> landlords in one dashboard: who has paid, who is owing, and the next renewal
> date, all in one place. You record a payment and the receipt is generated for
> you, so when a landlord asks, you have the answer in seconds, not hours.

Keep it to their world. Don't list every feature.

## 4. The demo offer (your strongest move)
> Want me to show you live? Takes about 10 minutes on a screen-share, I'll set up
> one of your properties with you so you see exactly how it'd work for your book.
> What time today or tomorrow works?

If they prefer to explore first:
> No wahala. You can start free here and add your first property in a few minutes:
> https://property360.africa?role=agent
> I'll stay on standby if you get stuck on anything.

## 5. Push to signup / trial
> It's free to start, no card needed. Add the properties you already manage today,
> and if a landlord later invites you to theirs, it lands in the same dashboard.
>
> Want me to walk you through the first property now?

Always end with a single clear next step (a link or a question), never a dead end.

## Objection handling

**"How much does it cost?"**
> You can start free and set up your portfolio without paying anything. There's a
> paid plan when you're ready for the full toolset, and I'll show you the options
> in the app, but nothing stops you trying it first. Want the link?

(Do not over-discuss price in chat. Get them in, let value sell the upgrade.)

**"Is my money / data safe?"**
> Completely. Payments run through Paystack, the same gateway your bank-grade apps
> use, and every action is logged. Nothing touches cash you can't trace.

**"My landlords won't use an app."**
> They don't have to do much. You manage everything from your side, and when you
> record a payment the landlord just gets a clean record and receipt. Many
> managers actually win landlords by showing up this organized.

**"I already use a notebook / Excel and it's fine."**
> That works until a landlord disputes a payment or you miss a renewal date. This
> does the same thing but keeps the proof for you automatically, so your word is
> never the only record. Try it free on one property and compare.

**"I'll think about it."**
> Of course. Can I set you up free now so it's ready when you want it? Takes 5
> minutes and costs nothing. Worst case you have it on standby.

**"Send me details / can I see more first?"**
> Here's a 90-second tour: https://youtu.be/jRnIUjQxVlk
> And you can start free here: https://property360.africa?role=agent
> I'm here if any question comes up.

**"Does it work on my phone?"**
> Yes, web, Android, and iOS. Use whichever you like, it's the same account.

## After they sign up (activation, do not skip)
The trial only matters if they add a property. Follow up same day:
> 🎉 You're in. Want to add your first property now? I can stay on chat and guide
> you step by step so it's set up properly. Send me the property name and rough
> rent and we'll do it together.

A manager who loads one real property in the first 24 hours is the one who stays.

---

## Lead tracking (do this manually for now)
Keep a simple sheet: name, phone, units managed, # landlords, status
(new / demo booked / signed up / activated / paid). It tells you which ad angle
and which audience actually convert, which is your real optimization signal at
this budget.
