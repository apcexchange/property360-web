# 60-second screen-recording script

A short video does more work than five screenshots ever will. This script is timed to **exactly 60 seconds** so it sends as one WhatsApp video without compression to a low resolution.

## What you need

- **Your phone.** No editing apps required. iOS built-in screen recorder works perfectly. (Long-press the screen-record button → enable Microphone.)
- **A test account** with at least one property, one tenant, one signed lease, and one recorded payment. Use realistic-looking but invented data.
- **Quiet room.** Background noise from a fan or TV gets picked up on phone mics.
- **Land in the app already signed in.** Don't waste 5 seconds typing a password on camera.

## Setup before recording

1. Open the Property360 mobile app or `https://property360.africa/app` in mobile Safari.
2. Land on **Dashboard**. Make sure today's date is visible — recipients trust dated screens.
3. Have the property detail page in your tab history (or one tap away).
4. Test your voice level once. Record 10 seconds, listen back, adjust your distance from the phone.

## The script (timed)

**0:00 – 0:05 · The hook (Dashboard view)**
> "This is Property360 — the dashboard a landlord sees when they sign in."

**0:05 – 0:15 · The pain you solve (still on Dashboard, point with finger)**
> "If you own a hostel or a few flats, you know the pain. Rent chasing. WhatsApp threads. Lost receipts. Property360 puts all of that in one app."

**0:15 – 0:25 · Show a property (tap into a property → unit list)**
> "Each building is one property. Inside, you can have ten rooms, twenty rooms, even a 40-room hostel — all under one property. Add your tenants once, and you're set."

**0:25 – 0:40 · Show a tenant + lease + payment (tap into a tenant)**
> "Each tenant has their lease, signed in the app — both you and the tenant. When they pay, it shows up here in real time. They get a receipt automatically. No more arguments about whether someone paid."

**0:40 – 0:50 · The price (cut to pricing page or speak it)**
> "Free for 14 days. After that, ₦2,250 a month for up to two properties. Less than the cost of a tank of fuel."

**0:50 – 1:00 · The ask (tap back to dashboard or hold camera steady)**
> "If you want to try it, the link is in the next message. Or just message me back — I'll add your first property for you. Thanks for watching."

## What NOT to do

- ❌ Don't read from a script visibly. If you have to look down, write 4 keywords on a Post-it next to your phone. Eye-line stays on the phone.
- ❌ Don't apologise ("Sorry it's a bit rough", "Excuse the background noise"). Recipients don't care. Just send.
- ❌ Don't say "Hi everyone" — it's going to be sent one-to-one, not broadcast. Talk as if you're addressing **one** person.
- ❌ Don't fake numbers in the demo. If your demo account has 3 leases, say "three leases". Real numbers — even small — beat invented bigger ones.
- ❌ Don't edit, music, or add captions. Send the raw screen recording. The roughness is the credibility.

## Iteration

Send your first 10 videos and watch the open + reply rates. After 10, adjust:
- If people reply with questions about pricing → spend an extra 5 seconds on that section next time.
- If people reply with "doesn't apply to me" → re-cut for a more specific audience (hostels-only, agencies-only).
- If nobody replies → your contact list is the wrong audience, not the video.
